    How long can I borrow a car?

    Unfortunately, cars can only be borrowed for 21 days. This period may be extended for one week at a time, with a maximum of three weeks. The extension is free and can only be done if no one else has made a reservation for that particular car. The condition of your cars can be viewed on your personal inventory account on our website.

    How can I reserve a car?

    Search for the car through your personal account on our website and click on the Reserve Car button. A reservations are valid for 7 days and cost $100 per car. If the car is not collected after 7 days, the reservation will be automatically canceled.

    What if i can't return a car on time?

    A fine of $10 per car is charged per day. When you keep in your possession a car that should have been returned, reservations cannot be made and new orders cannot be picked up.

    What happens if i lose a car?

    Please contact our customer service as soon as possible. Officials will charge a price for the car, based on on its value.
